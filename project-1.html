campaign to reduce vaccine hesitancy

In my PR Campaigns class, my team researched and designed a campaign to help reduce vaccine hesitancy in Hispanics in Tarrant County. At the time the campaign was produced, only about 45% of this group was fully vaccinated, compared to 65% of white Tarrant County residents.

I designed and developed a website for people to go to if they have questions or concerns about getting the COVID-19 vaccine. I also created fact sheets to be distributed to Tarrant County residents and leaders at various events.

project goals

1

Inform the target audience about the benefits of the vaccine.

2

Convince unvaccinated Hispanics in Tarrant County that the COVID-19 vaccine is safe and effective.

3

Increase vaccinations of Hispanic residents of Tarrant County.

4

Increase second dose vaccinations of Hispanic residents of Tarrant County.
