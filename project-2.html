social media and print ads for children's lighthouse

During my internship at Children's Lighthouse Franchise Company, I created several ads and collateral marketing materials for their 60+ early learning schools nationwide.

The print ad was designed to be mailed to families near the Children's Lighthouse Prosper location. It highlights the advantages of Children's Lighthouse over other preschools and childcare centers.

The social media ads were requested by owners at new locations who wanted to offer a discount to enrolling families. I included pictures of some of the students to help parents better envision their own children at Children's Lighthouse. The captions for the social media posts included instructions on how to enroll.

project goals

1

Attract new students and families to Children's Lighthouse locations.

2

Educate new parents on the benefits of Children's Lighthouse and its proprietary curriculum.

3

Increase web traffic for Children's Lighthouse Prosper.
