realeather website redesign

In my advertising campaigns class, my team designed a campaign to help Realeather reach more small business owners who make and sell leather products, such as Etsy sellers. We worked to convince these small business owners to choose Realeather over its competitors.

I redesigned Realeather's website home page to increase usability, update the brand's image, and include features that were not previously available, like a loyalty program and a custom products page. I also featured Etsy products that were made with Realeather to help drive sales for Realeather's loyal Etsy sellers.

Realeather's current site has a complicated navigation and organization system, too many hard sells, and outdated colors and textures. My proposed redesign aims to fix these problems in a way that is more functional and aesthetically pleasing for the target audience, who are mostly millennial women. The original site can be viewed here.

project goals

1

Increase usability and make the site more intuitive, especially on mobile.

2

Update Realeather's brand image to appeal more to the target audience.

3

Increase web traffic on pages for Etsy products featured on the site.

4

Increase the number of Realeather's repeat customers.
