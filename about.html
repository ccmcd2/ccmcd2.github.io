a strategic communicator
with an eye for design

I'm a strategic communicator and designer based in Arlington, Texas. I'm also an advertising, public relations, and communication technology student at the University of Texas at Arlington.

In my time at UTA, I have worked on several projects, including a campaign to reduce vaccine hesitancy in Tarrant County and a campaign to connect the Realeather brand with small business owners. I have work experience writing for and designing posters, brochures, websites, social media ads, print ads, and other marketing collateral.

Simply put, I love being creative. I have a passion for putting together my ideas and keeping an eye out for the small details that fall through the cracks. Some of my recent projects can be found here.
